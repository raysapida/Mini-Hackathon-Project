Sentimail helps you to write better e-mails.

Simply type your e-mail or copy an existing draft and click on 'Analyze'

Sentimail will analyze your draft on no less than 5 criteria:

Time To Read
How long it takes an average reader to read the message
Word Frequency
Overview of the top 5 most occuring words. This might indicate when synonyms are opportune
Formality Index
Indication of the formality level of your e-mail. Sentimail provides you with suggestions on how to make your e-mail more formal
Emotion Analysis
Analysis of the dominant emotions in the copy helps you to better conceive the message
(powered by IBM Watson™)
Tone Analysis
Analysis of the tone of voice to be more persuasive
(powered by IBM Watson™)
